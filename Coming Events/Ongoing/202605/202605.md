# Business Mission to China  
**Shanghai · Changsha**  
**From 25th to 29th May 2026**
---

A targeted, high-impact business mission designed for life science companies ready to take concrete steps into the Chinese market — whether you are looking for distributors, manufacturing partners, suppliers, co-development collaborators, or strategic investors.

We create **bespoke itineraries** that match your precise business objectives and leverage our deep, trusted network of industry players, key opinion leaders, and local government authorities — especially in Shanghai and the Yangtze River Delta region.

---
### What We Provide

- **Fully tailored mission program** — built around your shortlist of targets and priorities
- **Pre-qualified, high-level meetings** with vetted distributors, strategic partners, suppliers, CROs/CMOs, hospitals/KOLs, and investment groups
- **Direct introductions** facilitated through long-standing relationships with industry associations, development zones, and municipal government partners
- **On-the-ground support** — professional interpretation, local logistics, meeting facilitation, and cultural/business navigation

---
### Cost & Success-Based Structure

- Travel & accommodation fully covered by OctoBridge (flights, high-quality hotels, internal transport in China)
- Service & success fee payable only upon achievement of signed agreements during or directly resulting from the mission

This model significantly reduces your financial risk while aligning our incentives with your commercial success.
